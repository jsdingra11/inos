About Me

Astrophysicist | Science Communicator | Space Enthusiast

With 15+ years studying exoplanetary systems and galactic dynamics, Dr. Jane Cosmos has published over 50 peer-reviewed papers and given TED talks on cosmic phenomena. Currently leading the Exoplanet Discovery Project at the International Space Observatory.

Research Areas

Exoplanet Atmospheres

Analyzing atmospheric composition of planets outside our solar system using spectroscopy and transit photometry.

Galactic Evolution

Modeling galaxy formation through supercomputer simulations of dark matter distribution.

SETI Investigations

Leading an AI-powered search for non-random patterns in radio telescope data from the Kepler field.
